    About Me

    Interior Designer

    Education

    Bachelor of Interior Design

    I am an interior designer with a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from Amity University, Lucknow. My passion for creating functional and aesthetically appealing spaces has been shaped by my academic foundation and enriched through hands-on experience at Sandeep Chawla & Associates, a leading design firm in Greater Kailash II, New Delhi. At this esteemed firm, I had the opportunity to contribute to a diverse range of projects, gaining valuable insights into the intricacies of high-end residential and commercial interior design.

    Throughout my journey, I have developed a strong belief that interior design is much more than just creating beautiful spaces. It is a meticulous process that involves creative problem-solving, a deep understanding of spatial relationships, and the ability to integrate structural and technical knowledge seamlessly into the design process. My approach to design is centered on enhancing the functionality of spaces while ensuring that they reflect the unique personality and needs of the client. I am committed to providing solutions that are not only visually captivating but also practical, sustainable, and aligned with the latest design trends and innovations.

    As I continue to grow in this ever-evolving field, I am eager to learn while I collaborate with clients, architects, and fellow designers who share my passion for creating meaningful, innovative, and impactful spaces.
